Meetups

We had our first meeting on March 22, 2018, at {code}bridge in Newlands. We have not yet set a new date for our next meetup but we'll simply see what ideas come up over the coming weeks. Tentatively we can look into meeting up again in May 2018. Our idea is to rotate the venue of our meetups so that we don't favour a particular part of town. Do you have a venue you can offe or recommend? Contact us. Do you want to join our next meetup? Join our mailing list to stay up to speed.
