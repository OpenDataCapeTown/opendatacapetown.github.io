Join Us

We have formally launched this user group in March 2018, and we are always in search of new members. Do you have an interest in open data? Are you based in or interested in Cape Town's open data? Would you like to contribute ideas, time, data, code, or feedback? Then be sure to join us! There are two ways to contribute.

1. Working groups

We have outlined a number of working groups. These working groups consist of a group of people working around a specific topic. Each working group needs people to help achieve their goal, and for each of the groups there is a list with tasks that can be done. So if you are interested in contributing to a specific topic, then head over to the working groups!

Check out the working groups

2. Mailing list

More general discussions between all members of Open Data Cape Town takes place through an e-mail list. This Google Group is open to everyone and you can simply join it to join the discussion.

Join the group by sending a blank e-mail to: opendatacapetown+subscribe@googlegroups.com
