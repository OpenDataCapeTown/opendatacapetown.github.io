Homepage

Welcome to the website of Open Data Cape Town! We are a user group, which means that we are a community of people who are using open data that relates to the city of Cape Town (South Africa).

Our mission *

To improve access to and use of Cape Town's open data

We aim to do this by: *

  • Creating a community of users of Cape Town's open data
  • Lobbying for more and better open data
  • Distributing information about open data
  • Helping people use and understand open data

Open Data Cape Town is a recently created community (March 2018) and we are looking for YOUR input, ideas and involvement. So browse our site, learn more about what we do, and join us!

* We are still figuring out exactly what we do, so bear with us as we pinpoint our mission and vision. Consider these our temporary targets.
